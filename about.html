About mycompress

Redefining file management on the web.

Our Mission

At mycompress, our mission is simple: to provide the most powerful, secure, and user-friendly file manipulation tools on the internet—absolutely free of charge. We believe that basic document needs shouldn't require monthly subscriptions, hidden fees, or invasive data collection.

How It Works: 100% Client-Side

Unlike traditional online converters that force you to upload your sensitive documents to a remote server, mycompress utilizes cutting edge web technologies (like WebAssembly and Canvas APIs) to process your files directly inside your browser.

This means your files never leave your device. It's not just faster; it's a fundamental guarantee of your privacy.

Why We Built This

The internet is filled with "free" PDF tools that bait-and-switch users. You edit your document, only to find you hit a "daily limit," or you're required to create an account to download your file. We grew tired of this deceptive practice.

mycompress was built by a small, passionate team to offer an honest alternative. No limits. No sign-ups. No watermarks. Just pure utility.
