1. Introduction

Welcome to mycompress. We respect your privacy and are deeply committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle any information when you visit our website, regardless of where you visit it from, and tell you about your privacy rights.

Because mycompress relies on client-side browser technology rather than server-side uploads, our privacy footprint is exceptionally small. Read on to understand exactly how our tool operates.

2. Data Collection

When you use mycompress, we collect absolutely zero information regarding the files you compress, merge, convert, or otherwise manipulate. We do not require you to create an account, register an email address, or provide a payment method.

Like almost all websites, we may automatically collect standard anonymous analytics data to help us improve the platform. This encompasses browser type, operating system, and general geographic region (country level), but never personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Client-Side Processing

The core technology behind mycompress is known as "Client-Side Processing." This means that when you drag and drop a PDF, Image, or Video file into our tools, the file is never uploaded to an external server.

Instead, JavaScript libraries powered by WebAssembly run directly inside your web browser using your device's memory. Once the compression or conversion is complete, the file is saved locally to your hard drive. We technically have zero capability to view, store, copy, or share your files.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage strictly for functional purposes. For example, when you toggle the website's Dark/Light mode, we store that preference in your browser's local storage so the site remains in your preferred theme the next time you visit.

We do not use tracking cookies for targeted advertising or cross-site tracking. Any cookies used are strictly essential for the core functionality of the website.

5. Third Parties

mycompress utilizes reliable third-party Content Delivery Networks (CDNs), such as Cloudflare or unpkg, to serve the open-source libraries that power our tools (e.g., pdf-lib, ffmpeg.wasm). Your browser requests these libraries directly from the CDNs.

We will never sell, swap, rent, or otherwise distribute your personal data (should any be collected via a contact form) to a third party for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights under data protection legislation, including the GDPR or CCPA. Because our service processes your files locally and does not collect an account profile, there is typically no personal data held on our servers to request or delete.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights regarding personal data, please contact us via our Contact Page or email us directly at privacy@mycompress.test.
